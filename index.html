Quick Tips

Keep teeth healthy

Introduce tooth brushing gradually with finger brushes and tasty toothpaste made for cats.

Create vertical space

Shelves and tall cat trees help cats feel secure and enriched indoors.

Watch litter box habits

Changes in frequency or posture can signal medical concerns—don't ignore them.

Rotate toys

Keep playtime interesting by rotating a few toys weekly to rekindle curiosity.

Hydration is key

Some cats prefer flowing water—consider a cat fountain to encourage drinking.

Safe window views

Secure screens and provide cozy perches so cats can safely enjoy outside sights.
